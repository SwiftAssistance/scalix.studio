Featured Case Study: Wisetax Accountants

The Client: Wisetax Accountants

Wisetax is a modern accounting firm specializing in services for small businesses and freelancers. Their goal was to expand their digital reach and generate qualified leads from their target market.

The Challenge

Wisetax had a limited online presence and was struggling to attract new clients through digital channels. Their primary challenges were:

  • Low organic visibility on Google for key accounting services.
  • A lack of a consistent lead generation funnel.

Our Solution

Scalix Studios developed a comprehensive Google SEO strategy to help Wisetax Accountants find their target audience and grow their business.

Google SEO Strategy

We conducted comprehensive keyword research to identify high-intent search terms. Our work included:

  • On-Page Optimization: Rewriting website content and meta descriptions to rank for terms like "freelance accountant" and "small business tax services."
  • Content Creation: Developing a blog series focused on tax tips and financial advice for small business owners.
  • Technical SEO: Improving site speed and mobile-friendliness to boost search rankings.

The Results

Within a 3-month period, our SEO strategy led to significant improvements:

  • Increased organic website traffic by 45%.
  • Established Wisetax as a trusted industry expert with new online content.
